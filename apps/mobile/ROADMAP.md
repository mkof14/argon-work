# AGRON Mobile Delivery Roadmap

## Phase 0
- Marketing site live.
- Waitlist and lead capture active.

## Phase 1 (MVP)
- React Native app shell.
- Magic-link auth.
- Simulator drill runtime (single scenario).
- Score sync to `/api/simulator-score`.
- Pro entitlement gate.

## Phase 2
- Full LMS modules.
- Quizzes and progress dashboard.
- Completion certificates.

## Phase 3
- Corporate team dashboard.
- Seat assignment and team analytics.
- CSV/PDF export.

## CI/CD
- Lint, typecheck, tests.
- Build iOS + Android artifacts.
- TestFlight and Play Internal release.
- Staged rollout: 10% -> 25% -> 50% -> 100%.
